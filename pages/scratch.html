Our Story

  1. Who We Are:
    • First-generation Latino nursing students
    • Inspired by the sacrifices of immigrant parents
    • Committed to breaking barriers in healthcare
  2. Josh Solano
    • Born and raised in Orange County, California.
    • Parents immigrated from El Salvador as teens in search of better opportunities.
    • First-generation college student in his second semester of nursing school.
    • Driven to prove that minorities and first-gen students can achieve their dreams.
    • Motivated daily by his parents’ sacrifices and dedication.
  3. Bryan Conde
    • Born and raised in Danbury, Connecticut.
    • Parents immigrated from Ecuador in search of a better future.
    • Grew up with limited resources but strong family support.
    • First-generation college student in his fourth nursing semester.
    • Passionate about showing Latinos everywhere that success is within reach.
  4. Together as LosLatinoNurses
    • We honor our heritage and our parents’ sacrifices.
    • We aim to uplift and inspire Latino students in healthcare.
    • We share our journey to show that the world belongs to all of us.
    • We celebrate culture, ambition, resilience, and community.
